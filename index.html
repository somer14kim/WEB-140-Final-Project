Course Description

The Associates in Arts in Teacher Preparartion degrees allows North Carolina communitnity colleges to tranfer to a planned program of study with mininum of 60 semester credit hours.

The Associate in Arts Teacher Preparation degree is designed for students who coursework provides an in-depth understanding of reading, writing, oral communication, fundamental mathematical skills, and foundational computer use.
